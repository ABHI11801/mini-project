Popular Destinations

Agra

Home to the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort, Agra is a city steeped in Mughal history and architectural grandeur.

Varanasi

One of the oldest living cities in the world, Varanasi is a spiritual hub with its ghats along the Ganges River.

Amritsar

Known for the Golden Temple, Amritsar is a significant religious and cultural center for Sikhs.

Munnar

A picturesque hill station in Kerala, Munnar is famous for its lush tea gardens and scenic beauty.

Jaipur

The Pink City of Rajasthan, Jaipur is known for its historic palaces, forts, and vibrant culture.

Kashmir

Often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," Kashmir is known for its stunning landscapes, gardens, and valleys.
