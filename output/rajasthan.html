Rajasthan

Rajasthan, “Land of Kings”, is an Indian state located in the northwestern part of the country. It is India's largest state by area and is known for its historical forts, palaces, art, and culture.

Major tourist attractions include Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, and Jaisalmer.
